# AGENTS.md

This repo is a public-facing, site-neutral concept scaffold for a theoretical inland capsule hotel, civic simulation lab and health-surge support node on Minjerribah / North Stradbroke Island.

Use Australian English. Keep public wording practical, optimistic and careful. Do not imply approval, site control, clinical endorsement or a chosen address. Do not point the concept at any specific street address named in source documents or chat.

Public pages should explain the value a suitable space could create: local accommodation pressure relief, visitor flow data, civic simulation capacity, AI literacy, health-surge logistics, disaster readiness, workforce practice, co-op revenue and agent-readable planning records.

Keep private material, raw health data, family context, commercial terms, exact site candidates and unfinished negotiation notes out of public pages unless explicitly approved.

Builders should generate Markdown files for humans and agent teams. Keep copy/download/reset behaviour clear and browser-local.
