## Public Boundary

This site is not a chosen-site proposal. It does not direct the concept to any address. It should remain inland, site-neutral and review-gated until a real public/private process exists. The compute layer is also a planning model: idle GPU-hours and the roughly $3M AUD rack target need power, cooling, cyber, insurance, cultural, privacy and operator review before any procurement claim.
